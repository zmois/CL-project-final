About the Therapist

Stephanie Franz LMT, NCBTMB KY Lic. #5015

Stephanie began her massage therapy career in North Stonington, CT, where she graduated at the top of her class in 2005 from the Connecticut Center of Massage Therapy. With over 1000 hours of massage training, including a certification in Mother Massage (Pre-Natal, Post-Natal and Labor Support), she is also fluent in trigger point, Swedish, deep tissue, sports massage, myofascial release, reflexology and Medical Cupping.

She enjoyed a successful private practice in Westerly, RI for five years before relocating to Louisville. Clients appreciate her firm, strong touch, her intuition, and her specific knowledge of anatomy.

Stephanie believes in the body's ability to heal itself and that massage therapy is one of the many paths to restoring health and wholeness to the body.

Outside of massage therapy, Stephanie enjoys hiking, running, yoga, being a wife and mothering her son and daughter.
